Terms eLCA in beta

Please keep in mind that ELCA is currently in a beta stage.

The ELCA login information may not be disclosed.
Publications are permitted only with approval of the BBSR.
The product is in beta. Please report Identified errors.

Deposits into eLCA component templates are configured exclusively for the scope of the LCA exemplary constructions. The component templates are pre-use check-intensive. The component templates used must be adapted to each project to the specific circumstances.

Participants of the expanded beta be asked to gain insights on the following points.

Verification:

  • the calculation results
  • the stored useful lives
  • the deposited bulk densities
  • the stored conversion factors

Furthermore, they may like you create high-quality component templates submit for publication in the System.

Terms of Use

All recorded data can be scientifically evaluated by the BBSR. The evaluations are not project sharp and do not allow conclusions to be drawn individual projects. The evaluated data will be proportionately in totals and serve the formation of average values.

The evaluated projects serve the

  • formation of average values
  • Derivation of benchmarks
  • Identification of priorities and action areas

It is planned through regular publications, the findings to provide to the general public.
